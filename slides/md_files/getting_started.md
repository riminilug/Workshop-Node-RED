## Scrivere l'immagine su microSD


###The GNU/Linux way:
```dd bs=4M if=2017-01-11-raspbian-jessie.img of=/dev/sdX```

------

###The Mac OS way:
```sudo dd bs=1m if=path_of_your_image.img of=/dev/rdiskn```
